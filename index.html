Welcome to Alpha International School

We are a premier educational institution located in the heart of Rajpur Sonarpur, dedicated to providing quality education and a nurturing environment for students to thrive. Our motto, "Enter To Learn, Exit To Serve," guides our mission to develop honest, confident, and dynamic children.

About Our School

Today's schools do not guide children's moral development, but rather allow them to create their own moral structures and hierarchies. Thus, a growing need is reciprocated in every parent's heart to send their children to an Islamic School, to develop honest, confident and dynamic children in quest of knowledge for lifelong learning and serving with passion to excel at home, school and in society.

Alhamdulillah, this School has been established to bring out the best in your child, to live in a sanctuary where they can learn, worship, play and grow, free of social corruption, thus providing an ideal learning environment to enrich individual student's potential and imbibe Islamic values at an early age.
